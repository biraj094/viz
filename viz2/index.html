🎮 How to Use GeoScope

🎯 Basic Usage

  1. Click on any country to select it as your reference country
  2. Hover over other countries to compare their areas
  3. Click while hovering to pause on a particular comparison
  4. Click anywhere to resume comparison
  5. Click the Reset Map button to start over

✨ What You'll See

  • For Larger Countries: See how many times your selected country can fit inside
  • For Smaller Countries: See which combination of nearby countries equals your selected country's area

🎨 Visual Guide

  • Red - Selected country
  • Green - Combined countries (when showing equivalent areas)
