How to Use GeoScope

Select a Country

Click any country to set it as your reference. The panel shows its total area and how it ranks globally.

Compare Sizes

Hover over another country to see how it measures up:

  • Larger country — how many times your reference fits inside it
  • Smaller country — nearby countries are highlighted whose combined area matches your reference

Color Guide

  • Your selected reference country
  • The country you're hovering
  • Countries combined to equal your reference area

Freeze & Reset

Click while hovering to lock in a comparison. Click Reset to pick a new reference country.
